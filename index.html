📜 Golf Major Rules
  • 01Pick 4 golfers in order of expected finish. Only the top 3 count for scoring.
  • 02Lowest collective score to par across your 3 counting golfers wins.
  • 03You may pick a maximum of 2 of the top-10 OWGR players (as ranked the Monday of tournament week). You must pick at least 2 outside the top 10.
  • 04If 1 of your 3 picks misses the cut, your alternate (4th pick) is used in the 3rd scoring slot — it does not replace the MC player's position. Your two remaining active picks stay in their original order, and the alternate fills in as the 3rd scorer.
  • 05If 2 or more picks miss the cut, you are eliminated from the event.
  • 06GBC players: keeping you in the game. If 2 or more of your picks miss the cut, you're never eliminated — the GBC always plays on. To keep you at 3 active golfers, you'll receive a GBC Substitute: a stand-in scored at the worst active score among all GBC players' picks, plus 1 stroke. This keeps you competitive while fairly reflecting the impact of the missed cuts. Your alternate also activates for one of the MC slots, so you always have real golfers in the mix.
  • 06If duplicate picks occur (same order submitted), the earlier timestamp wins. The second better must resubmit.
  • 07In case of a tie on total score, it reverts to the finishing order of your individual golfers.

📅 Golf Betters Cup (GBC)

The GBC is a season-long competition for players who enter all four majors — the Masters, PGA Championship, US Open, and The Open. Your GBC score is the cumulative total of your final scores across all four events. Same entry fee ($200/major) and payout structure applies to the GBC at year-end.
